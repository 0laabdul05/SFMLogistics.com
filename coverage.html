Nationwide Coverage

From Lagos to Maiduguri, we’ve got you covered.

Where We Operate

SFM provides logistics solutions across all 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory, ensuring timely and reliable delivery to both urban and rural areas.

  • South West: Lagos, Ogun, Oyo, Osun, Ondo, Ekiti
  • South East: Abia, Imo, Enugu, Ebonyi, Anambra
  • South South: Rivers, Bayelsa, Delta, Edo, Cross River, Akwa Ibom
  • North Central: Abuja (FCT), Kwara, Kogi, Benue, Niger, Plateau, Nassarawa
  • North East: Adamawa, Borno, Bauchi, Gombe, Taraba, Yobe
  • North West: Kano, Kaduna, Katsina, Kebbi, Jigawa, Zamfara, Sokoto
