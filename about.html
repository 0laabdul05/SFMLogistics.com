About SFM

Delivering trust and speed across Nigeria since 2020

Our Story

SFM Logistics was founded to solve a common problem: unreliable delivery in Nigeria. We started with just 5 bikes and 2 trucks in Ilorin in 2020. Today, we run a nationwide network of vans, trucks, and motorcycles delivering thousands of parcels every week.

Our growth is driven by trust. From small business owners shipping products to students sending care packages home, we’ve earned loyalty by being fast, transparent, and dependable.

Mission & Values
